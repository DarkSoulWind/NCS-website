Team Accidents and Emergencies

We are team accidents and emergencies!

We are a group of teenagers aiming to raise money for the British Heart Foundation as part of our NCS Social Action Project, and we aim to do so by a scavenger hunt, designed by us!

The British Heart Foundation has helped halve the number of people dying from heart and circulatory diseases in the UK, but the sad reality is that everyday hundreds of people succumb to these crippling diseases. However with help from people like us who receive support from you the British Heart Foundation can continue to create new treatments and discover cures to win the war.

£24 could pay for two hours of research by an early career scientist, but every pound helps so if you could please help in whatever way you can it would mean a lot to us!
