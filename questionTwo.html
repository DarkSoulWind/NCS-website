Clue #2

What percentage of money donated to the British Heart Foundation goes to life saving research?





Spot on!

In 2018-19 the British Heart Foundation managed to raise more than £175 million.
£128 million was spent on life-saving research and support for heart patients, while £47 million was spent on the costs associated with generating income.

This means that for every £1 donated 72p went straight to funding research!

Incorrect!
